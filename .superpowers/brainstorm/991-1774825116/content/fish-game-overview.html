Key Elements

Player Fish

Swim with arrow keys/WASD. Eat clams, seashells, small fish. Grow bigger automatically. Jaw + health increase as you eat.

Sprint

Hold Shift to boost. 13-second stamina bar. Recharges when not sprinting.

Family

Toggle with F key. Smaller fish follow you in formation. They help eat nearby food. You start alone, gain family as you grow.

Shop

Spend coins to unlock new fish: Clownfish (free) → Bass (50🪙) → Megalodon (200🪙) → Mosasaurus (500🪙). Bigger fish start bigger.
