Mountain Hawk — Gameplay Mechanics

Physics, controls, scoring, and game states

Physics & Controls

Flight Physics
Falling — wings tucked, nose down
CLICK / SPACE / TAP → Flap!
Flapping — wings spread, nose up
Gravity: constant downward pull
Flap: instant upward velocity boost

Game States

1. Title Screen

Game title, hawk gently bobbing, "Click to Start" prompt. Wind audio playing.

2. Playing

Hawk flies, obstacles scroll, score increments on each pass. Full audio.

3. Game Over

Hawk tumbles, screen flash, final score + best score shown. "Click to Retry".

Scoring & Difficulty

Scoring

+1 point for each rock pillar passed. Best score saved to localStorage.

Difficulty

Classic Flappy Bird tuning — consistent gap size and speed. Tight but fair.

Collision

Bounding box on hawk vs rock pillars and ground/ceiling. Instant death on hit.

Controls

Click, spacebar, or tap (mobile) — all trigger a flap. Simple and universal.
