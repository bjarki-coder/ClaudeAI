Audio Design

Ambient Loop

Mountain wind — constant, gentle, with subtle volume variation. Plays on all screens.

Wing Flap

Short whoosh sound on each flap input. Procedurally generated with Web Audio API.

Score Ding

Soft chime when passing through a gap. Procedural tone via Web Audio API.

Eagle Cry

Occasional distant hawk screech at random intervals. Adds atmosphere without distraction.

Collision

Thud/impact sound on hitting rock or ground. Short and punchy.
