Key Elements

Player

A hawk with animated wing flapping — tilts up when flapping, angles down when falling

Obstacles

Snow-capped rock pillars forming gaps in mountain passes (replaces green pipes)

Background

Parallax layers: sky → far mountains → near mountains → pine trees → snowy ground

Atmosphere

Falling snowflakes, wind sounds, eagle cries, ambient mountain audio
